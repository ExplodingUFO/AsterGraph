## Behavior Added Or Tightened

- Avalonia node rendering now consumes `GetHierarchyStateSnapshot().Nodes` and only creates node visuals for nodes with `IsVisibleInActiveScope`.
- Collapsed node groups continue to render their group chrome while their member node visuals are omitted through the explicit `ProjectsMemberNodes`/hierarchy projection contract.
- Connection rendering now consumes `GetHierarchyStateSnapshot().Connections`:
  - internal connections hidden by the same collapsed group are not rendered;
  - boundary connections crossing a collapsed group remain rendered;
  - collapsed endpoints are anchored to the rendered group frame rather than to hidden member node visuals.
- Marquee selection now projects through the same hierarchy visibility and ignores nodes hidden by collapsed groups.
